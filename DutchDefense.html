The Dutch Defense

The Dutch Defense is a simple yet effective defensive opening for black

It is as follows; f5, NF6

The ideal setup for black while using the Dutch Defense is in the typical order of

f5, Nf6, g6, Bg7, O-O, d6

Overall, the Dutch Defense is a more radical approach against white's d4, however it can be a great way for black to respond a lot more safe

Have fun playing!
